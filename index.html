Meet Susan

What started as a university side hustle has blossomed into a full-fledged business built on passion, quality, and authenticity. Dulce's journey began with a simple love for creating beautiful handmade items and sharing delicious homemade treats with friends.

From beading intricate bags late into the night to perfecting her chinchin recipe, every product tells a story of dedication and craftsmanship. When she expanded into carefully curated imports, it was with the same commitment to quality that has always defined her brand.
